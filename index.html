About UNIOSUN

Osun State University — Centre of Excellence 🎓

Everything a 100L fresher needs to know — history, colleges, portals, scholarships & contacts.

Osun State University (UNIOSUN) was established in 2006, approved by NUC on 21 December 2006 as Nigeria's 30th state university and 80th in the national system.

It operates a collegiate system across 6 campuses in 6 geopolitical zones of Osun State: Osogbo (main), Ikire, Okuku, Ifetedo, Ipetu-Ijesa and Ejigbo.

VC: Prof. Odunayo Clement Adebooye  |  Pro-Chancellor: Prof. Wale Oladipo
